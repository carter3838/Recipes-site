How to fry eggs.

This will be easy:

  1. Put the pat on medium fire.
  2. Add some olive or sunflower seed oil on it.
  3. Break 4 eggs.
  4. Mix them so the egg yolk and egg white spread equally across pan.
  5. Put the lid on the pan.
  6. Mix eggs every 15 seconds.
  7. (Optional) Add some vegetables: corn, tomatoes(!), onion, garlic, you name it.
  8. Wait approximately 3 minutes, until eggs are ready.
  9. Add some cheese.
  10. Add some seasoning and salt.
  11. NOW you can start eating.
