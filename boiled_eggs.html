How to boil eggs.

There are three main types of boiled eggs:

  1. Soft-boiled eggs.
  2. Something in the middle.
  3. Hard-boiled eggs.

Algorithms Instructions:

  • Take pot and pour some water inside, so the eggs might be fully covered.
  • Put the pot on fire. On strong fire!
  • Wait until water boils.
  • Put eggs into water.
  • Set a timer:
    1. 4,5 min for soft-boiled eggs.
    2. 6 min for medium-boiled eggs.
    3. 8 min for hard-boiled eggs.
  • Take the eggs out of boiling water and put them into ice-cold water.
  • Wait for 2 min.
  • Eggs are ready!
